About The World Is Not Round

Ronnie and Chase had been writing music together for years, long before the conception of their band. Formed in 2013, theWorldisnotRound got its name spontaneously on a lazy Sunday afternoon in Chase's basement.

Fueled by their shared passion and love for music led them to discover their own style of indie with a unique blend of rock, mid-western emo, synth-wave, and pop. This sound is elevated and exudes an uplifting energy that goes beyond what is typically heard in the genre.

Albums

Since their debut, the group has put out an EP in 2014 called Random Electric EP, as well as two full-length albums: Alpha Rhythm in 2016 and oblatespheroid in 2018.

These records were well-received not only in their home city of Iligan, but also across the Philippines.
